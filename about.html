About

The people behind ELEKTRONN.

ELEKTRONN was created by Marius Killinger and Gregor Urban at the Max Planck Institute For Medical Research in Heidelberg an the Max Planck Institute Of Neurobiology in Munich in the department of Winfried Denk to solve connectomics tasks.
